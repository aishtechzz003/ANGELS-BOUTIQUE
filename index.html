Our Story

A Tradition of Trust.

Since the year 2000, Angels Boutique has been a beloved destination for women across Visakhapatnam seeking beautifully crafted garments for every occasion. What began as a small studio driven by passion for precision and design has grown into one of the city's most trusted fashion destinations.

At Angels Boutique, every garment is more than cloth and thread — it's a reflection of the woman who wears it. Our founder built this studio on one belief: every woman deserves to feel extraordinary in what she wears.

With over 25 years of experience and more than 333 reviews on Google, we've had the privilege of dressing thousands of women for their most cherished milestones.

Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your consultation with us.

How long does stitching take at Angels Boutique? +

The turnaround time depends on the complexity of your garment. Standard blouses are typically ready in 5–7 days. Bridal blouses and maggam work may take 10–15 days due to the intricacy involved. We always advise clients to book well in advance for wedding-related garments.

Do you offer bridal blouse stitching? +

Absolutely. Bridal blouse stitching is one of our most sought-after specialties. We work with all types of bridal fabrics including silk, kanjivaram, georgette, and more. We also offer bridal consultations to help you choose the right design for your saree and body type.

Can I bring my own design reference or inspiration images? +

Yes, absolutely! We encourage clients to bring reference images, Pinterest boards, magazine clippings, or any design inspiration they have. Our team will work with you to bring your vision to life while adapting it to suit your fabric, measurements, and preferences.

Do you provide maggam work embroidery? +

Yes, maggam work is one of our signature services. We offer a wide range of maggam work designs — from traditional patterns to contemporary styles. You can choose from various thread colors, bead types, and embroidery styles to create something truly unique.

Do you offer alterations for existing garments? +

Yes, we offer expert alteration services for all types of garments. Whether it's resizing a blouse, adjusting a salwar, or modifying an outfit to a new style, our team handles alterations with the same precision and care as our custom pieces.

How can I book an appointment with Angels Boutique? +

You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 93931 21303, sending us a WhatsApp message, or filling out the inquiry form on this page. We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks before your event to ensure adequate time for crafting and any adjustments.
